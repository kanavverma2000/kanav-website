What We Found

The NEM Generates 500,000 Data Points a Day.
We Made Them Talk.

THE CURTAILMENT CRISIS

SA solar is being wasted — 50.7% curtailed

South Australia throws away over half its solar generation. Victoria loses 27%. That's billions of dollars in clean energy never reaching consumers. ARIA shows exactly where and why.

CAPTURE RATIO GAP

Best wind farm earns 2x what the worst does

Kaban Wind Farm in QLD captures $1.12 for every $1 of average price. Stockyard Hill in VIC gets just $0.57. Same technology, wildly different returns. Location and timing are everything.

PRICE FORECAST

NSW prices could hit $109/MWh by 2030

Under a gas crisis scenario, ARIA's models show NSW wholesale prices climbing from $80 to $109/MWh. But if renewables boom, they could fall to just $51. The future depends on what we build next.

THE COAL CLIFF

12,910 MW of coal gone by 2035

Seven coal plants are scheduled to close, starting with Yallourn in 2028 and ending with Loy Yang A in 2035. That's nearly 13 GW that batteries, wind, and solar need to replace.

These aren't hypothetical. These are patterns ARIA finds in live AEMO data every single day.

What ARIA Delivers

Live NEM Monitor

Real-time spot prices across all five NEM regions via Open Electricity API. Implied pricing, generation mix donuts, spike detection, and auto-refresh every 5 minutes.

Curtailment Tracking

See exactly how much solar and wind is being wasted in each state — capped, curtailed, or hit by negative prices. Track lost MWh and estimated missed revenue.

AI Price Forecasting

Interactive scenario engine modelling prices to 2030. Adjust coal closure timing, renewable growth, BESS trajectory, gas prices, carbon price, and data centre demand.

💰

Revenue & Capture Rates

GWAP vs TWAP benchmarking, capture ratio rankings, price quintile heatmaps, and peer comparison for every wind, solar, battery, and hydro asset in the NEM.

🏭

Coal Exit Modelling

Track every scheduled closure from Yallourn (2028) to Loy Yang A (2035). Model price impacts, interconnector flows, and supply-demand rebalancing under bear/base/bull scenarios.

🔋

Battery Analytics

Dedicated BESS dashboard with charge/discharge cycles, utilisation factors, throughput metrics, daily dispatch profiles, and offtake contract intelligence.

Asset Explorer

Hydrogen & Ammonia

Green hydrogen project intelligence with electrolyser dispatch optimisation, LCOH calculator, ammonia economics, and forward cost projections tied to NEM prices.
