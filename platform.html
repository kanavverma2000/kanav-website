04 — Revenue Capture
Queensland wind farms earn more than the market. Victorian ones lose 24%.
A capture ratio of 1.0 means you earn exactly the market average. Above 1.0, you're beating the market. Below, you're underperforming. QLD wind farms average 1.08 — they generate when prices are high. VIC wind averages 0.76 — generating when prices crash. ARIA shows every asset where the money is going.

05 — Coal Exit Timeline
6,000 MW of coal disappears by 2029. What fills the gap?
Yallourn, Eraring, and Gladstone are all closing within three years — that's enough power for 4 million homes gone from the grid. ARIA models what happens to prices, reliability, and emissions as each plant exits. Spoiler: prices go up, and the race to build replacements is already behind schedule.
