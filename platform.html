01

Operational Analytics

Real-time monitoring of 500+ NEM generators across wind, solar, battery, and hydro at 5-minute dispatch resolution.

Asset Registry

Every DUID tracked by fuel type, region, capacity, and connection point. Fleet-level and technology-level aggregation.

Performance Metrics

Capacity factor, availability, curtailment rate, MLF impact, volume-weighted price — all at dispatch-interval resolution.

02

Revenue & Capture Intelligence

Understands how well every asset converts generation into revenue relative to market benchmarks.

Capture Price

Generation-weighted average spot price (GWAP) vs time-weighted average (TWAP). Measures revenue quality against baseload.

Revenue Decomposition

Spot revenue = output x price x MLF across every dispatch interval. Hourly, daily, monthly heatmaps and seasonal evolution.

03

AI Price Forecasting

Machine learning models that predict electricity prices hours and days ahead across all NEM regions.

XGBoost Quantile

P10/P50/P90 price forecasts using demand, temperature, solar/wind traces, interconnector flows, and time features.

Volatility Modelling

STL seasonal decomposition + EGARCH volatility clustering + Monte Carlo scenario generation for risk assessment.

04

Curtailment Attribution

Pinpoints exactly why renewable generators are told to switch off — and how much revenue they're losing.

UIGF Methodology

Uses AEMO's Unconstrained Intermittent Generation Forecast to measure lost output. Audit-grade P90 to UIGF correction.

Decomposition

Separates economic curtailment (negative prices), network constraints, and self-curtailment with rolling 30-day attribution.

05

Coal Exit & Market Intelligence

Models what happens to prices, reliability, and emissions as coal plants retire from the NEM.

Closure Scenarios

Replacement adequacy, regional price uplift, and CO2 intensity trajectories per sequential retirement scenario.

Constraint & Demand

Binding constraint frequency, shadow prices, interconnector flows, and regional demand patterns from 64M+ rows.

06

Investment Siting & ISP

Evaluates where new renewable projects should be built based on grid capacity, resource quality, and market opportunity.

REZ Assessment

AEMO ISP-aligned Renewable Energy Zone evaluation — resource quality, grid connection, development pipeline density.

New Entrant Economics

LCOE breakeven against forward price curves for wind, solar, and battery. Asset ranking by capture, capacity factor, revenue/MW.

Data Integrity

Validated at Every Layer

Every parquet file is validated for nulls, timestamp gaps, duplicate records, schema compliance, region consistency, and price bounds ($-1,000 to $20,300 MPC). Foundation lock protects 20 core files with SHA-256 integrity hashing.
